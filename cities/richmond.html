Official City Website

Founded in 1737, Richmond is known for it's deep and storied history dating back to before the American Revolution. In fact it was at St. John's Church in 1775 that Patrick Henry uttered the famous words "Give me liberty or give me death" ("give me liberty or give me death!" 2024). Today, Richmond serves as the states capital. With a bustling city center and quiet suburbs, Richmond offers the perfect blend of city living and the easy life.

Known for it's deep polictal history, Richmond continues to be an important hub in todays political landscape. As the capital of Virginia, many important leaders meet here every year to decide the future of the state.
