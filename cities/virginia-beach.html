Quick Facts

  • Founded: 1887
  • Total Population: 455,000( U.S. Census Bureau, 2024)
  • Region: Hampton Roads
  • Classification: Urban/Suburban
  • Average Household Income: $87,554, +$305 vs state average( U.S. Census Bureau, 2024)
Official City Website

Known for its sandy shorelines, Virginia Beach has been a popular destination for tourist and locals alike. Enjoy surfing, swimming, paragliding and more all on the ever famous Virginia Beach Boardwalk. In addition there is a lively and vibrant town center for shopping, dining, and entertainment.
